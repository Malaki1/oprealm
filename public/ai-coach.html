How it helps

Young creators get momentum when they feel stuck.

Kids often stop because the next step feels unclear. The AI Coach helps them shape ideas, keep characters consistent, make safer creative choices and move back into the right creation path, studio tool or reviewed library workflow.

Coach boundaries

  • Gives hints before answers
  • Explains code in plain language
  • Encourages screenshots and clear troubleshooting
  • Blocks unsafe personal information requests
  • Pushes creators back to safe OPREALM tools and reviewed library workflows

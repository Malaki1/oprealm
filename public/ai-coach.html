How it helps

Students get momentum when they feel stuck.

Kids often quit because they cannot find the next step. The AI Coach gives hints, checks understanding and points students back to the right lesson or safe community channel.

Coach boundaries

  • Gives hints before answers
  • Explains code in plain language
  • Encourages screenshots and clear troubleshooting
  • Blocks unsafe personal information requests
  • Pushes students back to moderated OPRealm spaces

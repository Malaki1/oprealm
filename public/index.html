Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents usually ask before joining.

Clear answers about safety, screen time, AI tools, community access, certificates, and what kids actually build.

Is OPRealm beginner friendly?

Yes. OPRealm was designed specifically for beginners aged 7-16.

Most students start with little to no coding experience. Lessons are taught step-by-step using fun, hands-on projects instead of overwhelming technical theory.

Does my child need coding experience before joining?

No prior experience is required.

We start with online safety, Roblox Studio basics, beginner controls, simple game building, and beginner-friendly coding concepts. Students gradually progress into more advanced creation skills as their confidence grows.

Is this just more screen time?

No. OPRealm focuses on turning passive screen time into creativity, problem solving, digital literacy, coding, design thinking, AI skills, and entrepreneurship.

Instead of only consuming games, students learn how to build, create, and publish their own.

How is OPRealm different from YouTube tutorials?

Most free tutorials are unstructured, overwhelming, outdated, unsafe, and difficult for kids to follow.

OPRealm provides structured learning paths, guided projects, moderated community spaces, AI creation tools, certificates, creator challenges, and beginner support. Everything is designed specifically for younger learners.

Is the Discord community safe for kids?

Safety is one of our highest priorities.

Our community includes moderated channels, strict anti-bullying systems, filtered language moderation, age-appropriate spaces, controlled AI tool access, and private member-only areas. Students must complete the Safety Academy before accessing the broader creator community.

What devices are supported?

Most students use Windows PCs, Macs, or laptops capable of running Roblox Studio or browser-based tools.

Some beginner content may work on tablets, but a computer is recommended for the best experience.

What will my child actually create?

Depending on the course, students may create obstacle courses, adventure worlds, racing games, AI story games, mini-games, custom Minecraft experiences, trailers, game assets, soundtracks, and sound effects.

Advanced students may even publish and monetize their own games.

How do the AI tools work?

Members receive monthly Creator Credits that can be used inside our Discord community to generate game trailers, sound effects, music, thumbnails, characters, textures, sprites, and game assets.

The tools are designed to encourage creativity while remaining beginner-friendly and safe.

Can my child publish their own game?

Yes. Students learn how publishing works, game thumbnails, beginner marketing, monetization basics, and sharing games responsibly online.

Elite Creator students receive additional publishing and launch guidance.

How much time should my child spend each week?

Most students spend 2-5 hours per week.

Because courses are self-paced, students can learn around school and family schedules.

Are lessons live or self-paced?

The Creator Membership and Creator Pro tiers are primarily self-paced.

The Elite Creator Intensive includes live workshops, guided mentorship, project feedback, and cohort-based learning.

What if my child gets stuck?

Students can access community help channels, AI Coach support, walkthrough lessons, and troubleshooting guides.

Creator Pro and Elite members receive additional support access.

Will my child receive certificates?

Yes. Students can earn completion certificates, creator badges, achievement ranks, and community recognition.

These help motivate progress and celebrate milestones.

Is OPRealm educational?

Yes. OPRealm develops coding fundamentals, logical thinking, creativity, storytelling, problem solving, AI literacy, digital citizenship, and entrepreneurial thinking.

All through project-based learning kids actually enjoy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Memberships can be cancelled anytime directly from your account dashboard.

What age group is OPRealm designed for?

Most courses are designed for ages 7-16.

Some beginner-friendly content is suitable for younger creators with parent guidance.

Why teach kids AI tools?

AI will become a major part of future careers and digital creativity.

We teach kids safe AI usage, creative prompting, responsible creation, and problem solving with AI. Instead of passively consuming technology, they learn how to use it creatively and responsibly.

What makes OPRealm unique?

OPRealm combines game development, AI creation tools, online safety education, moderated community, publishing guidance, and creative entrepreneurship into one platform built specifically for kids and teens.

Build the games they wish existed, safely.

Email Support

General support: support@oprealm.com

For now, use the same address for safety, privacy, billing, and partnership enquiries.

What to Include

  • Your name and parent email address.
  • The student display name or safe alias, if relevant.
  • The membership tier or creation path.
  • A short description of the issue or question.
  • Screenshots where helpful, especially for technical or safety reports.

Safety Concerns

If a student feels unsafe, they should stop replying, keep communication inside moderated OPRealm spaces, take a screenshot if possible, use the report tools, and tell a trusted adult.

Response Times

During early access, response times may vary. Safety and account access concerns are prioritised.

Using OPRealm

OPREALM provides creator tools, AI tools, reviewed public marketplace features, and reusable asset systems for young creators. Parent or guardian involvement is required for children under the age of majority in their location.

Safety Comes First

Young creators may need to complete required safety awareness guides before accessing broader AI tools or public publishing features. OPREALM may restrict, suspend, or remove access where safety rules are broken.

Creator Behaviour

  • Be kind, respectful, and age-appropriate.
  • Do not bully, harass, threaten, or shame other members.
  • Do not ask for DMs, friend requests, social handles, phone numbers, school names, passwords, or private contact details.
  • Do not share unsafe links, scams, free Robux claims, or inappropriate content.
  • Keep projects, prompts, comments, and uploads suitable for children and teens.

AI Tools

AI tools are provided to support creativity. Creators must use AI responsibly and may not use it to create unsafe, hateful, sexual, violent, bullying, private-information, or rule-bypassing content. AI outputs may be imperfect and should be reviewed before sharing or publishing.

Creator Credits

Creator Credits are internal OPRealm usage credits for AI tools. They are not cash, cannot be transferred outside OPRealm, and may expire or reset depending on membership tier. Tool costs may change as provider costs and features change.

Payments and Memberships

Paid memberships may renew monthly unless cancelled. Elite Creator is a premium creator support offer with separate scheduling and availability where applicable. Pricing, inclusions, and availability may change for future customers.

Projects and Content

Creators keep ownership of their original project ideas and creations where allowed by the tools and platforms they use. OPREALM may display creator work in the reviewed public marketplace only where appropriate permission is provided. OPREALM may also retain generated props, decor, templates, style recipes and reusable assets in its internal asset library to improve future procedural creations and platform tools.
